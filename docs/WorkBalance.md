WorkBalance™ – Project Documentation
1. Project Overview
   WorkBalance™ is an employee-centric therapeutic support platform designed to improve emotional resilience, mental clarity, and workplace well-being. It operates as the employee-facing pillar of the broader HumanOps ecosystem, focusing on intervention rather than surveillance. The system provides guided psychological support without diagnosing or treating mental illness.
2. Core Philosophy
- Employee psychological safety is non-negotiable
- Employers see outcomes, not personal content
- Therapy-inspired, not therapy-replacing
- Work-context aware interventions
- Ethical by design
3. Target Users
   Primary Users:
- Employees (all roles)

Secondary Stakeholders:
- HR Managers
- Team Leads
- Company Owners

Note: Employers never access individual-level content.
4. Employee Workflow
1. Secure login (company invitation)
2. Daily emotional check-in
3. Situation selection (stress, conflict, fatigue, etc.)
4. Guided therapeutic path
5. Reflection and closure
6. Progress overview
7. Optional follow-up reminders
5. Main Pages – Employee Side
1. Welcome & Onboarding
- Psychological safety explanation
- Privacy promise

2. Daily Check-in
- Mood scale
- Energy level
- Short optional note

3. Situation Selector
- Deadline pressure
- Emotional exhaustion
- Feeling undervalued
- Conflict with colleagues
- Lack of motivation

4. Therapeutic Path Pages
- Grounding
- Emotional labeling
- Cognitive reframing
- Micro-action suggestion

5. Reflection Summary
- What changed?
- Emotional intensity before/after

6. Progress Dashboard
- Emotional trends
- Completed paths
- Personal insights

7. Settings & Privacy
6. Therapeutic Path Structure
   Each path contains:
- Validation statement
- Regulation exercise
- Cognitive insight
- Behavioral micro-step
- Closure message
7. Example Therapeutic Paths
- Deadline Pressure
- Burnout Risk
- Workplace Conflict
- Anxiety Before Meetings
- Loss of Motivation

8. Employer Visibility (Indirect)
   Employers see only aggregated, anonymized data:
- Overall engagement rate
- Organizational stress trend
- Burnout risk index
- Department-level well-being movement

No individual data is ever accessible.
9. Ethical & Legal Positioning
   WorkBalance™ is positioned as:
- Emotional support system
- Mental resilience platform
- Psychological hygiene tool

It explicitly avoids diagnosis, treatment, or medical claims.
10. Business Value for Companies
- Reduced burnout
- Improved emotional regulation
- Fewer conflicts
- Increased focus and stability
- Healthier work culture
11. Monetization Model
- B2B SaaS
- Per employee pricing
- Monthly or annual billing
- Optional enterprise customization
12. MVP Scope
- 5–7 therapeutic paths
- Text-based guidance
- Basic progress dashboard
- Employer outcome dashboard
- Strong privacy communication
13. Future Expansion
- AI-assisted adaptive paths
- Voice-guided exercises
- Therapist marketplace

14. Final Notes
    WorkBalance™ is designed to become a trusted emotional infrastructure inside modern organizations. Its value comes from restraint, ethics, and clarity — not complexity.
